Help Center

What is Gadget Grid?

Gadget Grid.com is a tool for online comparison shopping. We make it surprisingly easy for you to find a product, know about that product and compare it with others. Use Gadget Grid to quickly obtain free and unbiased information about mobile phones before making a purchase decision.

How do I compare things side-by-side?

Creating a side-by-side comparison on Gadget Grid is a great way to directly compare detailed information for various products. To create a side-by-side comparison click the compare button on any product page or product listing. Continue to select products until you have collected all of the products you want to compare. Now click compare now button to compare the products you have selected. You can also view and modify products in your compare list by clicking on compare queue in the user menu.

How do I login?

To login first click on the user icon (on the top right corner). Now select login from the user menu and login using facebook or google

How do I sign up?

To sign up first click on the user icon (on the top right corner). Now select sign up from the user menu and sign up using facebook or google. Don't forget to verify your email after signing up.
